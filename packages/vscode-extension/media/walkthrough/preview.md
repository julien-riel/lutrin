## The preview recompiles as you type

`Lutrin: Show Presentation Preview` opens the deck beside the text
(also the preview icon in the editor title, or `Ctrl+K L` / `Cmd+K L`).

- The panel follows your cursor from slide to slide.
- Only slides whose rendering changed are replaced — scrolling and
  animation state survive each keystroke.
- Click an animated slide to step through its reveals, exactly as
  PowerPoint will play them.

The preview is the compiler's real HTML output, not an approximation:
what you see is what exports.
